Animation & Game Development Production

Diploma / Advance Diploma / BCA Animation & Game Development Production (Delhi & Chandigarh)

Diploma / Advance Diploma / B.Sc Animation & Game Development Production (Bhopal)

This Program is designed for those who think in beats, visuals, and energy. This hands-on course is your creative playground—where you’ll learn to design impactful soundtracks and effects, direct and produce visually compelling music videos, and master industry-standard audio tools and music tech. From studio work to real-world shoots, you’ll collaborate with artists, DJs, and creators on actual music projects. Whether you're building beats or creating the next viral video, this program equips you with the skills, tools, and experience to bring your sound and vision to life.

Eligibility Criteria

12th pass from any stream, UG students, or candidates with relevant experience.

Course Duration

3-Year degree in line with NEP & NCrF
(Multiple Entry & Exit Options)

Training Hours

500 hours per Year

Jain Online
